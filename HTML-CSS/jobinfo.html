Paleontologists

Description

A paleontologist is a scientist who studies fossils and contributes to research on their historical, sociological and archeological impacts. These individuals often specialize in a specific era or type of fossil, which may influence some of their typical tasks.

Average Salary

In Halifax, NS, the average salary for a paleontologist is around $73,146 per year or $35 per hour. Entry-level positions start at around $39,500 annually, while top earners can make up to $129,000.

Employers

Paleontologists can find employment in diverse sectors, including universities, museums, research institutions, government agencies, and even consulting firms specializing in environmental or geological studies

Related Careers

Related careers to paleontology include museum curator, science writer, park ranger, archaeologist, science teacher, biologist, and research scientist, all leveraging similar scientific skills and interests
