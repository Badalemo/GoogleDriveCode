Lenses: Seeing Small and Seeing Far

And Just What is a lens?

A lens is a curved surface that refracts light with the purpose of bringing the light to a focal point. Forms of lenses have been around since at least the age of Ancient Egyptians - 600BC!

Microscopes

A microscope allows you to see things that are VERY, VERY small. Some microscopoes, called optical microscopes, use lenses to focus light into your eye to form an enlarged version of the thiny thing at which you look.

Telescopes

A telescope allows you to see things taht are FAR, FAR away. Some telescopes use lenses to pull light together to form the image of the faraway object. Other telescopes use mirrors to accomplish the same task.
